About the project

E-commerce analytics is the process of gathering data from all areas that have an impact on your online store and using this information to understand the trends and the shift in consumersâ€™ behavior to make data-driven decisions that will drive more online sales. In E-commerce, the current challenge is determining how to provide a sustainable competitive advantage through a better understanding of target customers. In this project we have used proper visualization of data so as to find proper statistics of how the region and the products for sale have performed and we can take necessary actions to improve the same. We have also shown how discount and sales have affected the profit.
